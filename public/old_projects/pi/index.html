This website cycles throught the first million digits of pi, which were accessed from here, every 24 hours. It starts at 12:00 AM every day.
This was mainly inspired by pi videos such as Numberphile's Mile of Pi, and other interesting websites about pi such as the Pi-Search Page by Dave Andersen

Currently, I am working on implementing a feature that shows when points of interest will appear in pi, such as the Feynman point and the world record for memorization. I am also working on controls to go to a specific time, cycle through digits manually, and find other ways to explore pi. I also want to work on performance and maybe use more server-size stuff rather than downloading a 1mb text file when the page loads.
